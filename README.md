# Smart-City-Parking-EV-Charging-Optimizer
The Smart City Parking &amp; EV Charging Optimizer is an intelligent IoT and software solution designed to streamline urban mobility. It maximizes parking efficiency and electric vehicle (EV) charging availability by providing real-time data to drivers and city operators.

The ProblemDrivers in major cities lose significant time and fuel searching for parking spaces. Simultaneously, the lack of real-time visibility into EV charging station availability exacerbates driver "range anxiety" and causes gridlock at high-demand charging hubs.
